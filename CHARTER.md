# The PUNN Charter

## Purpose

PUNN Cognitive Architecture (PCA) exists to advance human understanding through the design of trustworthy cognitive systems.

PCA is not created to replace human intelligence.

PCA is created to strengthen it.

Its purpose is to transform artificial intelligence from answer generation into understanding generation.

---

# Mission

To establish an open, transparent, and evidence-guided cognitive architecture that enables humans and intelligent systems to reason together responsibly.

---

# Vision

To become a foundational cognitive architecture for human-AI collaboration across education, science, healthcare, government, industry, and society.

---

# Core Beliefs

PCA is founded on the following beliefs.

• Understanding is more valuable than answers.

• Reasoning is more valuable than prediction.

• Evidence is more valuable than confidence.

• Transparency is more valuable than persuasion.

• Human agency is more valuable than automation.

---

# Principles

Every implementation of PCA shall preserve the following principles.

1. Purpose before response.

2. Understanding before generation.

3. Evidence before confidence.

4. Transparency before persuasion.

5. Human agency above automation.

6. Continuous learning through evidence.

7. Architecture before implementation.

---

# Scope

PCA is a cognitive architecture.

It is not

• a language model

• a prompt library

• a chatbot

• a software framework

• a programming language

Instead,

PCA defines the principles and architecture upon which intelligent systems can be designed.

---

# Success Criteria

PCA succeeds when it enables humans to

• understand reality more accurately

• reason more transparently

• make better decisions

• learn continuously

• preserve independent judgment

---

# Governance

PCA shall evolve through evidence, criticism, experimentation, and open collaboration.

No implementation, contributor, or technology shall supersede the constitutional principles of the architecture.

---

# Commitment

PCA remains open to revision.

No model is final.

No architecture is beyond improvement.

Only evidence has the authority to change the future of PCA.

---

# Final Statement

The purpose of intelligence is not to think for humanity.

The purpose of intelligence is to help humanity think better.
